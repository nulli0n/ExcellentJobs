# About

**ExcellentJobs** is a lightweight and modern jobs plugin with high customization and unique features.

### 10+ Premade Jobs

Contains **well-balanced** ready-to-play jobs out of the box!

Including Miner, Lumberjack, Fisherman, Farmer, Baker, Butcher, Animal Keeper, Monster Hunter, Enchanter, Alchemist.

### Leveling System

Built-in optional **leveling system** with level GUIs, featuring **geometric** and **linear** progressions.

**Flexible rewards** with customizable **conditions** and commands that support **placeholders**.

### Contracts System

Let players sign **contracts for jobs** to get various **income bonuses** in exchange for a specific price and work time.

Get **promotions** in your contracts and **unlock new ones** with better terms for you!

### Grind System

Create your own sources of **xp**, **money**, and **contract points** based on player actions.

Supports **many action types**, including integration with **MythicMobs**, **CustomCrops**, **InfiniteFishing**, and others.

### Job Zones

Create **work zones** with renewable blocks, where players get a **bonus or penalty** to money and xp.

Set **access conditions** for these zones, like job level or specific **permissions**.

### Leaderboards

Built-in **leaderboard** with a GUI and **PlaceholderAPI** support.

Track your **stats** and compete with other **players**!
